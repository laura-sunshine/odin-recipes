Tofu Stirfry

A delicious tofu and vegetable stirfry, served with rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • Block of firm tofu (around 400g)
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 120ml water
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • Any stirfry veg
  • Couple of handfuls of cashew nuts
  • Sprinkle of sesame seeds
  • (Optional) 1/8 teaspoon cayenne power

Method

For the marinade

  1. Mix all the tofu marinade ingredients in a mixing bowl until well combined. Add the tofu cubes and let them marinate in the fridge for at least 15 minutes.
  2. Drain the tofu but don't discarb the liquid, set aside
  3. Heat the oil in a wok or frying pan until golden brown
  4. Add the marinade liquid to a bowl along with the cornstarch and mix until well combined. Pour the sauce into the pan and cook until it thickens.

Tofu marinade recipe

Rest of the dish

  1. Depending on which vegetables you have chosen, you may need to part boil them before adding them to the pan
  2. Once everything has cooked and the sauce absorbed, serve in dishes
  3. Sprinkle some sesame seeds on top and a handful of cashew nuts
